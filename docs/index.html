大多数想法，死在动手之前

不是因为不会写代码，而是因为根本没搞清楚要做什么。

I have an idea but I don't know if it's worth building."

我有个想法，但不知道值不值得做。"

想做 MVP，但不知道从哪里下手。"

跟 5 个人讲了我的想法，得到了 5 种不同的意见。"

How It Works
产品流程

Clarity in 30 minutes

30 分钟，从模糊到清晰

A structured AI conversation that asks the right questions — and challenges you when your answers are vague.

结构化 AI 对话，问你真正该想清楚的问题 —— 并在你回答含糊时直接追问。

1

Share your idea

说出你的想法

Start with whatever you have. Rough is fine. The AI knows how to dig deeper.

有什么说什么，哪怕很粗糙。AI 会引导你深入挖掘。

2

Answer 7–10 questions

回答 7–10 个问题

Who's the user? What pain exists? How do they solve it today? Why is your approach different?

用户是谁？什么痛点？现在怎么解决的？你的差异化在哪？

3

Get challenged

被追问和质疑

The AI doesn't just agree. It pushes back on vague scope and identifies real risks.

AI 不会一味迎合，会对模糊的范围提出质疑，指出真实风险。

4

Receive your MVP plan

获得 MVP 方案

A clear, structured plan: problem, user, feature scope, what's out, risks, first action.

一份结构清晰的方案：问题、用户、功能范围、边界、风险、第一步行动。

5

Connect with the founder

联系创始人

Confirm when you're ready to move forward. A real human reaches out to help you ship.

确认方案后，真人创始人会主动联系你，帮你把产品落地。

What You Get
你会得到什么

A real plan, not just advice

是方案，不是泛泛的建议

MVPilot produces a structured output you can actually use — not a list of generic tips.

MVPilot 输出的是结构化的产品方案，可以直接拿来用 —— 不是一堆笼统的建议。

Your MVP Plan includes:

MVP 方案包含：

✓

Problem Statement

问题陈述

One clear sentence. Who has what pain.

一句话说清楚：谁有什么痛点。

✓

Target User

目标用户

Specific, not "everyone who uses phones".

具体的人，不是"所有用手机的人"。

✓

MVP Feature Set

MVP 功能清单

3–5 things to build. That's it.

3–5 个功能，仅此而已。

✓

Out of Scope

明确不做什么

Explicitly what NOT to build in v1.

v1 明确不做的功能，写清楚。

✓

Top 3 Risks

前三大风险

Technical, market, and execution risks.

技术、市场、执行三个维度的风险。

✓

First Action

第一步行动

The single most important next step.

最重要的那一个下一步动作。
